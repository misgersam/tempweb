Financial
Services
Client is one of the largest financial services
mortgage company in the United Kingdom
Situation
As a result of past CRM failures, the mortgage client was left with several custom legacy CRM systems, which were expensive to maintain, swivel chairing selling experience, prospects ending up in DNC due to lack of a comprehensive 360 view
Solution
We reviewed and evaluated the client’s existing system and offered
a strategic approach that not only provides a foundation to support
existing business process, but also embraces a Customer centric
approach vs former lead centric approach. We enabled a capability
that can consume enterprise analytics to enhance sales effectiveness
for L1 and L2 agents (Loan Officers). 

                      Here are few key drives that contributed greatly to the project:

                      
    
                        
  •  Single view of the customer, that includes all existing loans and 
    
                          existing loan application without missing any activity updates. 
    • 
                        
  •  All info in one place ( single source of truth )
    • 
                          
  •  30 % Improved lead conversion
    • 
                            
  •  Guided flows to officers for next best actions(NBA) to close the deals
    • 
                            
  •  Reduced the callback time to customer to less than 5 seconds
    • 
                        

                 

              

        

  
  
  

      


          

            Benefits



                
    
                  
  •  Effective Lead Prioritization improving the efficiency of the Sales team 
    • 
                  
  •  Automatic assignment of leads to Loan Officers
    • 
                  
  •  Automatic conversion of leads into opportunities /Accounts / Contacts
    • 
                  
  •  Improved LO onboarding time by 50 percent
    • 
                  
  •  Fully integrated Sales tool with a unified view of the application workflow in Salesforce
    • 
                  
  •  Successfully integrated the Salesforce system with different third-party systems (as required by the client) to process requests
